About Advocate Sowjanya

Advocate Sowjanya is a qualified legal practitioner with a passion for justice, fairness, and client welfare. Practicing primarily in Consumer Disputes, Civil and Property Matters, Family Law, and Legal Documentation, she brings a thoughtful and client-centered approach to every case.

Her philosophy is simple: Listen carefully, understand deeply, and act with integrity.

With experience representing clients before district forums and various legal authorities, Sowjanya is known for her calm demeanor, transparent communication, and genuine care for her clients' concerns.

She believes that legal guidance should be accessible, clear, and empowering, not intimidating or confusing.

"Every client deserves to be heard and guided with honesty — not just represented in court, but supported in every step."

Sowjanya continues to expand her practice, offering both in-person and online consultations to make legal help available to all across Telangana.
